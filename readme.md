## About LSTM

LSTM are a special kind of Recurrent Neural Network, capable of learning long-term dependencies. They were introduced by [Hochreiter & Schmidhuber (1997)](http://www.bioinf.jku.at/publications/older/2604.pdf), and were refined and popularized by many peoplo in following work [[Sundermeyer et al. (2012)](https://www.isca-speech.org/archive/interspeech_2012/i12_0194.html), [Zhou, Chunting, et al. (2015)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1511.08630), [Gensler, Andr√©, et al. (2016)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/7844673)].

All recurrent neural networks have the form of a chain of repeating modules of neural network. LSTM also have this chain like structure, bellow follow the module in an LSTM.
